# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Code-Notify to be a respectful and productive open source project. Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate clearly, assume good intent, and avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of differing experience levels and viewpoints.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and the technical issue at hand.
- Give constructive feedback and accept feedback professionally.
- Report security issues privately instead of opening public exploit details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption of issues, discussions, or pull requests.
- Any behavior that makes participation unsafe or hostile.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may temporarily or permanently block contributors who violate this code of conduct. For sensitive reports, contact the maintainer through the email listed on the npm package or use GitHub's private security reporting when applicable.
